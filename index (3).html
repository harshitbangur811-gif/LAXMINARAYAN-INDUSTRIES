About Us

A Foundry
of Precision

Laxminarayan Industries is a specialist metal foundry located at Plot No. 1401, Vatva GIDC Phase 3, Ahmedabad. We operate a 130 kg electric induction furnace, enabling precise temperature control, clean melts, and consistent metallurgical quality across every heat.

We serve clients across pumping systems, Indian Railways, and street infrastructure — industries where dimensional accuracy, structural integrity, and surface finish are non-negotiable. Every casting that leaves our shop floor meets the exact specification it was ordered to.

Our three casting processes — sand casting for complex geometries, shell mould casting for superior surface finish and dimensional accuracy, and mould casting for high-volume components — give clients the right process for every requirement.

Materials

Alloys We Cast

Our electric induction furnace gives us precise temperature control, enabling consistent work across ferrous and non-ferrous alloy families.

Grey Cast Iron (CI)

Excellent compressive strength and machinability. The workhorse for pump bodies, manhole covers, and machine bases.

Ductile Iron (SGI)

Spheroidal graphite iron with superior tensile strength and impact resistance. Preferred for railway and high-load applications.

Aluminium Alloys

Lightweight, corrosion-resistant castings for pump impellers and components requiring low weight with good strength.

Copper-Base Alloys

Bronze and gunmetal castings for bearings, bushings, valve seats, and applications requiring corrosion resistance.

Special Alloys

High-chromium, ni-resist, and specialty grades for abrasive, high-temperature, and corrosive service environments.

How We Work

The Casting Process

From pattern to finished casting — a disciplined sequence ensuring dimensional accuracy, structural soundness, and surface quality at every stage.

01

Pattern & Tool Design

We work from customer drawings or samples to produce sand moulds, shell mould tooling, or permanent metal moulds with correct draft and shrinkage allowances.

02

Charge Preparation

Raw materials are weighed and batched to the target alloy specification, ensuring consistent chemistry in every heat.

03

Induction Furnace Melting

Our 130 kg electric induction furnace delivers precise temperature, clean melts with minimal slag — critical for SGI nodularisation and aluminium alloys.

04

Sand · Shell · Mould Casting

Sand casting for complex geometries. Shell mould casting for superior finish and tighter tolerances. Mould casting for high-volume runs.

05

Shakeout & Fettling

Castings are broken out after controlled cooling, runners and risers removed, surfaces fettled and dressed to specification.

06

Inspection & Dispatch

Dimensional and visual inspection before dispatch. Shot-blasting, painting, or machining performed to customer requirement.
